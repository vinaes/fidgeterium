Privacy Policy

Fidgeterium · Last updated: July 3, 2026

Short version: Fidgeterium does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Everything the app remembers stays on your device.

Data we collect

None. Fidgeterium has no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, and no third-party SDKs. The app does not send any information about you off your device.

Data stored on your device

To make the app work, the following is saved locally on your device only (and never transmitted to us or anyone else):

  • Your settings (sound, cube orientation, selected skin, and preferences).
  • Your achievements and progress.
  • Your custom skin, if you create one.

Removing the app removes this data.

Purchases

The optional one-time “Pro” unlock is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your name, payment details, or Apple ID. Purchase and restore are handled by Apple’s StoreKit; please see Apple’s Privacy Policy for how Apple handles that.

Children

Fidgeterium is safe for all ages. Because it collects no data, it collects nothing from children either.

Third parties

We do not share data with third parties, because we do not collect any. The only party involved in a purchase is Apple.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.

Contact
